# Audio sources

  https://opengameart.org/content/simple-menubackground-music-loop
- `game_music.mp3`: "Tower Defense Theme" by DST, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/tower-defense-theme
- `zombie_spawn.mp3`: "Zomby sfx pack" by saturn91, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/zomby-sfx-pack
- `final_wave.mp3`: "Basic Sound Effects" by n4, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/basic-sound-effects
- `attack_skewer.ogg`, `attack_wing.ogg`, `attack_meatball.ogg`: "Rpg Sound Effect Pack" by Delta12 Studio, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/rpg-sound-effect-pack
- `attack_beef.wav`: "Ice spells" by bart, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/ice-spells

Attribution is not required by CC0, but the source information is retained for project traceability.
