# Audio sources

All downloaded audio in this folder is released under CC0 / public domain terms.

- `menu_music.ogg`: "Simple menu/background music loop" by polosik, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/simple-menubackground-music-loop
- `game_music.ogg`: "Tower Defense Theme" by DST, OpenGameArt; converted from MP3 to OGG for browser compatibility.
  https://opengameart.org/content/tower-defense-theme
- `zombie_spawn.ogg`: "Zomby sfx pack" by saturn91, OpenGameArt; converted from MP3 to OGG for browser compatibility.
  https://opengameart.org/content/zomby-sfx-pack
- `final_wave.ogg`: "Basic Sound Effects" by n4, OpenGameArt; converted from MP3 to OGG for browser compatibility.
  https://opengameart.org/content/basic-sound-effects
- `attack_skewer.ogg`, `attack_wing.ogg`, `attack_meatball.ogg`: "Rpg Sound Effect Pack" by Delta12 Studio, OpenGameArt.
  https://opengameart.org/content/rpg-sound-effect-pack
- `attack_beef.ogg`: "Ice spells" by bart, OpenGameArt; converted from WAV to OGG for browser compatibility.
  https://opengameart.org/content/ice-spells

Attribution is not required by CC0, but the source information is retained for project traceability.
